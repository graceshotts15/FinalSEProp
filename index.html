PC Shopping

Creators: Grace Shotts, Ethan Kohl, Praise-God Nkalang'ango, Ahmad Saleh

Application Overview

A website where you are able to shop different types of PC builds, ranging from very good to some cheaper designs. Be able too add a item to cart and "check out" the PC you want to buy, and be able too see details about what products are in the PC and how good/bad they are.

Technology Overview

Hosted on azure, written in HTML and JavaScipt. Utilizes CSS.
